Get started

Need to send a courier urgently? Don’t worry — sending your parcel through DTDC Courier is quick, secure, and easy. Whether it’s a document, gift, or online order, DTDC Kavi Nagar, Ghaziabad makes the process smooth from start to finish.

Step 1: Pack Your Parcel Properly

Start by packing your parcel securely. Use a strong box or envelope and seal it properly with tape. For fragile items, use bubble wrap or cushioning material. Clearly write both the sender’s and receiver’s address on the package.

💡 Pro Tip: Mention your mobile number on the parcel for easy contact during delivery.

Step 2: Visit DTDC Kavi Nagar Branch or Request Pickup

You can either visit our office located at Shop No. 1, near Nagar Nigam Office, Opp. Old Kacheri, Block D, Sector 18, Kavi Nagar, Ghaziabad or call us for doorstep pickup. Our staff will help you choose the right courier plan based on your parcel type, weight, and destination.

Step 3: Fill Out the Shipment Form

Provide details like sender and receiver information, destination pin code, and parcel type. Our team will generate an AWB (Air Waybill) number — this is your tracking ID to monitor delivery progress online.

Step 4: Choose Your Delivery Type

  • Express Delivery – Fastest delivery option for urgent parcels.
  • Economy Service – Affordable choice for non-urgent shipments.
  • International Shipping – Send parcels abroad safely and quickly.

Step 5: Make Payment & Get Receipt

Once the parcel is weighed and details are confirmed, make the payment. We accept cash, UPI, or card. You’ll receive a booking receipt containing the AWB number.

Step 6: Track Your Parcel

After dispatch, you can track your shipment anytime on the DTDC Tracking Page using your AWB number. You’ll see real-time updates until the delivery is completed.

📦 Tip: For business users, DTDC also offers regular pickup scheduling and bulk shipment options.
